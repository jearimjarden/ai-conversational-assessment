# AI Conversational Assessment Agent

## 1. Background

Many property owners have difficulty identifying operational problems and understanding how AI and automation can improve their business.

Currently, this assessment is performed manually by consultants through meetings or interviews. The process is time-consuming, inconsistent, and difficult to scale.

An AI conversational assessment agent can automate the initial consultation by collecting business information through natural conversation and generating a structured assessment report with recommendations.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Current challenges include:

- Manual assessment requires significant consultant time.
- Information collected varies between consultants.
- Property owners often do not know where to start.
- Consultants spend too much time gathering basic information before providing recommendations.
- There is no standardized assessment process.

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-powered conversational assessment agent that:

- Conducts natural conversations in Indonesian.
- Collects business information step-by-step.
- Extracts structured information automatically.
- Tracks assessment progress.
- Identifies missing information.
- Generates a business assessment report.
- Recommends AI and automation opportunities.

---

## 4. Target Users

Primary users include:

### 1. Idle Property Owner

Owns a property but it is not currently generating income.

Examples:

- Empty villa
- Vacant apartment
- Unused boarding house
- Closed hotel

---

### 2. Operating but Underperforming

Currently operates a property but experiences business challenges.

Examples:

- Low occupancy
- High operational cost
- Manual workflows
- Poor customer experience

---

### 3. Growing Property Owner

Already operates successfully and wants to improve efficiency or expand operations.

---

## 5. Business Objectives

The AI should help:

- Reduce consultant assessment time.
- Standardize information collection.
- Improve recommendation quality.
- Identify automation opportunities.
- Improve customer experience.
- Increase consultant productivity.

---

## 6. Expected Outputs

At the end of the conversation, the system should produce:

- Property profile
- Business assessment
- Operational analysis
- Technology assessment
- AI readiness evaluation
- Automation recommendations
- Next action plan

---

## 7. Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful if it can:

- Conduct a complete assessment conversation.
- Extract structured business information.
- Store assessment data.
- Generate a meaningful assessment report.
- Recommend relevant AI solutions.
